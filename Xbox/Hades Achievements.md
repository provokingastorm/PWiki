

### Arms Collector
Unlock each of the Infernal Arms
https://www.trueachievements.com/a330994/arms-collector-achievement#41123

 - Focus on collecting keys
 - Keys can be spent in the Training Room to buy weapons

**From Hades Wikia**
**Weapons** are your main mode of attack. Initially, only the [Stygian Blade](https://hades.fandom.com/wiki/Stygian_Blade "Stygian Blade") is available, but additional weapons can be unlocked by spending [Chthonic Keys](https://hades.fandom.com/wiki/Chthonic_Key "Chthonic Key") in the Arsenal Room. Unlocking all the weapons requires a total of 24 [Chthonic Keys](https://hades.fandom.com/wiki/Chthonic_Key "Chthonic Key").
