
## Farming Scrolls
For any of you wanting a specific scroll, the best way I found is to equip the drop charm on your main turtle (the higher the % the better, I use a 25%), also equip an item master charm and a haggler charm on a support turtle. Play in hard, once you get the hang of the best charms it isn't that difficult.  
  
-If you want to farm for stone warriors scrolls the best level is Wingnut, keep circling the highest tower with your main turtle and takedown (one hit kill) any warrior who has'nt notice you (question mark over their heads).  
  
-If you need a specific boss scroll, General Krang is the best level. And to enhance the chances to get the scroll from the bosses (Guide or Compendium) you need to get an S rank on every battle. The easiest way to get an S rank is to buy from Splinter the turret with the turtle that has the haggler charm before each boss fight, and use it on the boss with the turtle that hast the item master charm, be sure that the turtle is not Leonardo because you are gonna need to use the turret in tandem with turtle time to rack the most damage and combo hits possible. One last thing is to remember that you must land the last hit on the boss with the turtle that has the drop charm.  
  
This strategy applies to every boss encounter, and it helps a lot because you can drop about 3 health bars in the first seconds of the fight. It has help me to get an S rank on every level

## Farming Mousers
To get Mouser Scrolls in _Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles: Mutants in Manhattan_, you need to **defeat Mouser enemies and bosses** (such as the Mother Mouser or Big Mouser) during your missions. They spawn frequently in sewer levels and during boss fights. [[1](https://support.activision.com/teenage-mutant-ninja-turtles--mutants-in-manhattan/articles/teenage-mutant-ninja-turtles-mutants-in-manhattan-gameplay-faq), [2](https://www.facebook.com/groups/BoardGameBBQCommunity/posts/2473584286431592/), [3](https://turtlepedia.fandom.com/wiki/Teenage_Mutant_Ninja_Turtles_III:_The_Manhattan_Project), [4](https://steamcommunity.com/app/338400/discussions/0/364042703857921457/), [5](https://turtlepedia.fandom.com/wiki/Teenage_Mutant_Ninja_Turtles_\(2003_video_game\))]

Keep in mind that these items are tied to specific enemies: [[1](https://support.activision.com/teenage-mutant-ninja-turtles--mutants-in-manhattan/articles/teenage-mutant-ninja-turtles-mutants-in-manhattan-gameplay-faq)]

- **Scrolls:** Drop regularly from standard enemies.
- **Guides & Compendiums:** These are rarer, upgraded versions of scrolls. To get them, you will generally need to play on **higher difficulty levels** to trigger them to drop. [[1](https://support.activision.com/teenage-mutant-ninja-turtles--mutants-in-manhattan/articles/teenage-mutant-ninja-turtles-mutants-in-manhattan-gameplay-faq)]
