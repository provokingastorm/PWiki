
## Cleaning TVs
### Do's
 - Dust the TV first.
 - Use a **damp** microfiber towel with water to clean most the TV.
 - Clean with up-and-down strokes.
 - Distilled water is the safest liquid, if you have it.
 - Use the [[#TV Cleaning Solution]]

### Don't's
 - Never spray the TV (or other screens) directly with liquids. Spray the towel.

### TV Cleaning Solution
An effective cleaner for getting rid of greasy marks on electronic screens is to mix this solution in a reusable spray bottle:

 - 1 cup of distilled water
 - A few drops of dish soap
