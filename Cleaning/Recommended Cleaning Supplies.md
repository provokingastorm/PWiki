
## Scrubber

**Scrub Daddy/Mommy**
They do  a great job scrubbing. Can be warmed up to softened for delicate items. You can send in old scrub daddies to the company for recycling to get a little store credit to replace the ones you used. 

**Drill Brush Scrubber**
These are used for tougher dirt spots. It saves you physical effort in scrubbing hard. Ryobi is a good brand. The batteries are reusable with their other tools. There are other brands as well, like Hoover. 

## Spray Bottles

**Echo Lab**
A little more expensive than a dollar store spray bottle, but still cheap. Found at Home Depot. 

## Towels

**Microfiber Towel**
By far, the best product for repeat uses. Use gloves while using microfiber towels if you dislike the feeling of microfiber towels on your skin.

**Terry Cloth**
An alternative to microfiber towels. 

**Huck Cloth**
An alternative to microfiber towels. Also called surgical cloths.
