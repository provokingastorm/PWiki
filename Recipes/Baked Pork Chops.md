#Video

**Cooking Steps**
 - Pre-heat the oven to 400 degrees.
 - Cook for 15-20 minutes or until a thermometer reads 145 degrees  [02:12](https://www.youtube.com/watch?t=132&v=RHtdY-qjLKw)

**Optional Step**
 - Broil pork chops for the last minutes of cooking to get a nice caramelization at 140 degrees. [02:19](https://www.youtube.com/watch?t=139&v=RHtdY-qjLKw)
 - Spray on some olive oil before broil if pork chops are looking dry.
