Best practices on reheating common frozen food or leftovers.

### PF Chang's Honey Chicken

 - Start the sauce before cooking the chicken.
 - Heat sauce in glass bowl on top of pot filled with water (steam the sauce).
 - Cook the chicken in an air fryer for 7 minutes, not 9 minutes as mentioned in the instructions, at 400 degrees (9 minutes nuked the chicken to 200 degrees).
