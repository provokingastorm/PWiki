#Breakfast
### Equipment

 - Waffle maker

### Ingredients

 - Kodiak waffle mix
 - Olive oil

### Instructions

1. Apply olive oil spray to waffle maker.
2. Pre-heat the waffle maker.
3. Pour 1 cup of Kodiak mix in a mixing bowl.
4. Add 1 tbsp of olive oil to the bowl.
5. Add 3/4 cup of water to the bowl. 
6. Stir thoroughly until combined well.
7. Pour on waffle maker when it's done pre-heated.
