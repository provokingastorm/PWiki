### Equipment

 - Air fryer

### Ingredients

 - Baby potatoes.
 - 1 tbsp olive or avocado oil.
 - 1/4 to 1/2 tsp of salt.
 - 1/2 tsp of garlic powder.
 - 1/2 tsp of Italian seasoning.
 - 1/2 tsp of chopped rosemary (fresh is best, dried is fine too).
 - 1/4 tsp of black pepper. 

### Instructions

 - Wash and cut baby potatoes.
 - Place in a large mixing bowl.
 - Add oil (olive or avocado) to potatoes. 
 - Add salt, pepper, garlic powder, rosemary to potatoes.
 - Shake to combine. 
 - Preheat air fryer to 375 degrees. 
 - Put coated baby potatoes in the pre-heated air fryer. 
 - Cook for 10 minutes. 

### Notes

 - Replace Mrs. Dash with salt when cooking for Mom.  
 - The original instructions called for 385 degrees. However, that seemed too hot.
 - Also, the original instructions called for 15 minutes of cooking in the air fryer. After 10 minutes, they seemed done. Cooking for longer may burn them.
