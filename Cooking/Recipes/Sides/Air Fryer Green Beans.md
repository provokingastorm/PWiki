### Equipment

 - Air Fryer

### Ingredients

 - Green Beans
 - Olive Oil
 - Garlic
 - Salt, Pepper, Mrs. Dash
 - Lemon (for zest)

### Instructions

 - Cut the ends and clean the green beans.
 - Press the garlic.
 - Drizzle oil on green beans in a bowl.
 - Add spices. (Do NOT add garlic yet).
 - Pre-heat air fryer to 375 degrees.
 - Cook the green beans for 7-8 minutes.
 - Halfway during the cooking, shake the basket, then add the garlic (see Notes section for why adding garlic here).

### Notes

 - I tried the original recipe where I added the garlic initially with the spices. However, the garlic was almost burned by the halfway cook time. Instead of reducing the cook time, adding the garlic halfway through could give you crispy green beans from the long cook time without burnt garlic.
