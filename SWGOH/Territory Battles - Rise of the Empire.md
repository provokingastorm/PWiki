### Mustafar Planet

#### Mustafar Combat Missions

**Fleet Battle**

*Starting Line-up*
 - Leviathan
 - Scythe
 - Mark VI Interceptor
 - B-28 Extinction-class Bomber

*Reinforcements (in order to be called)*
 - Sith Fighter
 - Scimitar
 - TIE Dagger
 - TIE Advanced x1

*Notes*
 - When deciding who to stun from the reinforcements, target a vulture droid to be stunned so it doesn't assist. 
 - Prioritize targeting other ships (except for vulture droids) over Hyena Bomber since that's just a tank ship. 
 - Put the stealth ability on Mark VI Interceptor.
