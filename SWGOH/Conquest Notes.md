These are general purpose tips on achieving better results in Conquest mode. These tips won't guarantee that you get the Red crate or even the Gold crate. To achieve maximum potential, you will need a plan based on the Conquest feats. Those vary from Conquest season to season.

## Top General Tips

 - Always find at least one node per sector that has enemies with no AOE abilities. Makes it easier to complete "Win with X unit surviving."
 - Always be on the hunt for at least one node per sector with a [[Character - Droideka|Droideka]] or [[Character - Kylo Ren (Unmaksed)|Kylo Ren (Unmasked)]].
 - [[Character - Wat Tambor|Wat Tambor]] and [[Character - Jedi Master Luke Skywalker|Jedi Master Luke Skywalker]] is a Conquest MVP pairing. They keep weak units alive with taunt and call them to assist to get kills.
 - Data disks are always key for a super easy Conquest. You should set to change your disks to optimize performance. When in doubt, refer to the [[#Frequent Best Data Disks]] section.
 - For ultimate Conquest laziness, Padawan Obi-Wan omicron is a massive time saver. 
 - The F3-AT data disk allows you to never kill the enemy team. Making it easy to auto-farm debuff and buffs. It saves you a ton of energy.
